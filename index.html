About

I am an incoming Lecturer of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology.

I completed my Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University, where I was advised by Dr. Yuejie Chi. I received my B.S.E. with Honors in Electrical Engineering from Arizona State University with a Minor in Mathematics.

While at CMU, I was the sole recipient of the Oustanding ECE Teaching Assistant Award for the 2018-2019 Academic Year for my contributions to 18-290 in Fall 2018 and 18-202 in Spring 2019.

During my Ph.D., my research was focused in the design and analysis of efficient algorithms for solving inverse problems--especially within the realm of image reconstruction.
