About

I am an incoming Lecturer of EECS at MIT.

I completed my PhD in ECE at CMU, where I was advised by Dr. Yuejie Chi. I received my BSE with Honors in EE from ASU with a Minor in Math.

While at CMU, I was the sole recipient of the Oustanding ECE Teaching Assistant Award for the 2018-2019 Academic Year for my contributions as Head TA of 18-290 in Fall 2018 and co-Head TA of 18-202 in Spring 2019.

During my PhD, my research was focused on the design and analysis of efficient algorithms for solving inverse problems--especially within the realm of image reconstruction.
